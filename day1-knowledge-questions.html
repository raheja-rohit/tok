Dealing with Knowledge Questions in the Subject Guide

  • Don’t deal with KQ’s in an abstract way
  • Link the KQ’s with appropriate examples from your subjects
  • It is a good idea to start with an example/question from your subject to a TOK question rather than asking the TOK question directly
  • Move between First Order and Second Order thinking
  • Explore ways in which the KQ can help you initiate deeper thinking about your subject using Knowledge Framework.
  • Ask students to give examples / counterexamples related to the KQ
  • Use these KQ’s as big questions in your unit planner
  • Explore interdisciplinary connection using KQ
